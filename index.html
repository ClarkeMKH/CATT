Meet the trainer behind the treats

Kind, qualified support for real dogs and real life.

Hi, I'm Chloe, and I believe in positive reinforcement training. I am passionate about helping dogs and their owners build lasting, trust-based relationships.

My training methods are force-free, reward-based, and adapted to suit your dog's personality. Whether you're working on puppy foundations, lead skills, recall, reactivity, confidence building, or muzzle training, I am here to guide you every step of the way.

My dog, Zuri, is the reason behind everything I do. She is reactive through fear to certain things, and this inspired me to start training. Through our journey, we built a deeper connection, and I found my passion for helping other dogs and owners do the same.

I offer 1-2-1 dog training and hope to one day expand my business to include a secure dog field and an indoor training space, providing a safe and enriching experience for every dog.

I am a registered dog trainer with the Animal Behaviour & Training Council (ABTC), with over six years of hands-on experience working with and training dogs through two of the UK's largest animal welfare charities.

Our Philosophy

Why positive reinforcement works

Reward-based training isn't just kinder — it's more effective. When dogs associate learning with good things, they want to engage, try harder, and repeat the behaviours we're looking for. No fear. No force. Just trust.

Builds confidence, not compliance

Dogs learn to think, problem-solve, and engage — not just react to avoid consequences.

Strengthens your bond

Training becomes something you both look forward to — not something to dread or push through.

Safe for every dog

Especially beneficial for nervous, anxious, or reactive dogs who need gentleness above all else.

I love what I do

I've learnt so much over the years and would love to put this knowledge to good use — training together and having a good time while achieving your goals.
